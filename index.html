About

Turning ideas into polished products

An iOS-first developer who also ships AI-powered web and mobile experiences.

I'm Mohammed Ajas, an iOS developer and software engineer who designs and builds native Apple apps — and modern web & mobile products with AI at their core. I help startups and businesses transform ideas into polished, production-ready products.

My main expertise is iOS development with Swift and SwiftUI. I also specialize in AI applications, LLM integrations, and the Model Context Protocol (MCP) — connecting large language models to real tools and data. Alongside that, I build workflow automation and full-stack systems using Flutter, Next.js, Node.js, and well-designed REST APIs.

My focus is always the same: clean architecture, thoughtful UX, and software that genuinely makes work easier.
